The BMW m3 f80 BMW is the car brand. m3 is the car line. f80 is the chassis code.


The BMW m3 f80 is an amazing car for its price. It's stock 0-60 time is 3.9 seconds and for a car produced between 2014 and 2018, that's pretty quick.

The f80 is offered with 2 transmissions: DCT(dual clutch transmission) or a 6-speed manual.

It's also rear wheel drive, which means it's easy to do donuts in.

Although the average cost for a f80 in good condition is around $60k, it is my most realistic dream car.
